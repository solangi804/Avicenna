About Our School

Empowering Minds, Shaping Futures

Our Mission

At Avicenna Grammar School, we believe in nurturing young minds through a balanced approach to education that combines academic excellence with character development. Our mission is to provide accessible, quality education that empowers students to become confident, responsible, and successful individuals.

Our Vision

Founded with the vision to provide accessible quality education in Ahmed Pur Lamma, our school follows the Oxford Curriculum to ensure international standards of learning. We envision a learning environment where every child can thrive, discover their potential, and achieve their dreams.
