Admission Process

We are now accepting applications for the 2026 academic year for classes from Play Group to 8th Class. Our admission process is designed to be simple and transparent.

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Visit Our School

    Schedule a campus tour to see our facilities and meet our staff.

  2. Submit Application

    Complete and submit the admission application form along with required documents.

  3. Required Documents
    • Birth certificate (original and copy)
    • Previous school records (if applicable)
    • Two recent passport-size photographs
    • Parent/Guardian CNIC copy
    • Medical certificate (if required)
  4. Assessment/Interview

    Attend an informal assessment or interview (if applicable for higher classes).

  5. Admission Decision

    Receive admission decision within 3-5 working days.

  6. Enrollment

    Complete enrollment process and fee payment to secure admission.
