About Our School

Avicenna Grammar School is committed to providing quality education that empowers young minds and shapes their future.

Our Mission

At Avicenna Grammar School, we believe in nurturing young minds through a balanced approach to education that combines academic excellence with character development.

Founded with the vision to provide accessible quality education in Ahmed Pur Lamma, our school follows the Oxford Curriculum to ensure international standards of learning.

We cater to students from Play Group to 8th Class, providing a strong foundation for their future academic and personal growth.
