Our Educational Programs

Comprehensive programs designed for different age groups and learning needs

Early Years Program (Play Group - KG)

Our early years program is designed to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment for young learners. We focus on developing foundational skills through play-based learning and interactive activities.

Key Features:

  • Play-based learning approach
  • Development of motor skills
  • Social and emotional development
  • Introduction to basic literacy and numeracy
  • Creative arts and crafts activities
  • Safe and secure learning environment

Age Groups:

Play Group (2-3 years), Nursery (3-4 years), KG (4-5 years)

Primary Program (1st - 5th Class)

Our primary program builds strong academic foundations with a focus on literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills. Students develop a love for learning through engaging and interactive lessons.

Key Features:

  • Strong foundation in English, Mathematics, and Science
  • Oxford Curriculum-based learning
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Reading and writing development
  • Computer literacy and technology integration
  • Regular assessments and progress tracking

Subjects Offered:

English, Mathematics, Science, Urdu, Social Studies, Islamic Studies, Computer Studies, Arts, Physical Education

Middle School Program (6th - 8th Class)

Our middle school program prepares students for higher education with specialized subject knowledge and skill development. We focus on academic excellence and character building.

Key Features:

  • Advanced subject knowledge
  • Preparation for board examinations
  • Research and project-based learning
  • Leadership and teamwork skills
  • Career guidance and counseling
  • Extracurricular activities and clubs

Subjects Offered:

English, Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Urdu, Social Studies, Islamic Studies, Computer Science, Arts, Physical Education
